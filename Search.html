Search Key Legend

  • Type: t:dragon or type:artifact
  • Mana Color: c:r (red), c:wu (white/blue)
  • Mana Value: mv=3, mv<=2 (also supports cmc)
  • Edition (Set): set:neo, set:mh3
  • Name: name:"lightning bolt" or plain lightning bolt

Combine keys like t:creature c:g mv<=3 set:bro
